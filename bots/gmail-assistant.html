How It Works

1. Enable the Assistant

Once installed, open Gmail and start a new email. The Gmail Assistant icon will appear in your compose window.

2. Describe Your Intent

Tell the assistant what kind of email you want to write, such as "follow up on client meeting" or "send project update".

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gmail Assistant free to use?

+

Gmail Assistant offers a free tier with basic features like grammar checking and simple suggestions. Advanced features like tone analysis and professional templates are available in our premium plan.

Does the assistant have access to my emails?

+

The assistant only accesses the current email you're composing to provide suggestions. It doesn't read or store your inbox contents or sent emails. All processing happens locally on your device for maximum privacy.

How accurate are the AI suggestions?

+

Our AI model is trained on professional business communications and achieves over 95% accuracy in grammar and style suggestions. The more you use it, the better it adapts to your personal writing style.

Can I use this with email clients other than Gmail?

+

Currently, Gmail Assistant is optimized for Google's Gmail interface. We're working on versions for Outlook, Yahoo Mail, and other popular email clients that will be available soon.

How do I uninstall the assistant if needed?

+

You can disable or uninstall the assistant like any other Chrome extension. Go to Chrome's extension management page, find Gmail Assistant, and click "Remove" or toggle the enable switch.
