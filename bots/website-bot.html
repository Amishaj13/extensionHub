Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the bot's answers?

+

The bot provides answers based directly on the content of the webpage you're viewing. Its accuracy depends on the information available on the page. It won't make up information that isn't there, and it will let you know if it can't find an answer to your question.

Does the bot collect my browsing data?

+

No. Website Bot processes all page content locally in your browser. We don't track your browsing history, store the content of pages you visit, or send your queries to our servers. Your data stays private on your device.

What types of websites does it work with?

+

Website Bot works with most public websites including news sites, blogs, documentation, e-commerce sites, and educational resources. It may not work properly with sites that have heavy JavaScript rendering, password-protected content, or dynamically loaded content.

Is the extension free to use?

+

Yes, Website Bot is completely free for basic usage, which includes unlimited questions on public webpages. We offer a premium plan with additional features like PDF analysis, custom site indexing, and API access for power users.

Does it work in languages other than English?

+

Currently, Website Bot supports English, Spanish, French, German, and Japanese. We're actively working on adding more languages. The bot will automatically detect the language of the webpage and respond in the same language when supported.
