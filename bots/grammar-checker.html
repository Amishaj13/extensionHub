How It Works

1. Install the Extension

Add the Grammar Checker to your browser with one click from the Chrome Web Store. No account creation required to start.

2. Start Writing Anywhere

The assistant works automatically on any text field. As you type, it underlines potential errors and suggests improvements.

3. Review Suggestions

Hover over underlined text to see detailed explanations of the issues and suggested corrections.

4. Apply Corrections

Click on suggestions to apply them instantly, or open the assistant panel for a comprehensive review of all issues.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this grammar checker better than built-in spell checkers?

+

Yes! Unlike basic spell checkers, our AI-powered grammar assistant can detect contextual errors, suggest style improvements, and understand the meaning behind your sentences to provide more accurate suggestions.

Does it work in all languages?

+

Currently, our grammar checker fully supports English (US, UK, Australian, and Canadian), Spanish, French, and German. We're actively working on adding more languages based on user demand.

Is my writing data secure?

+

Absolutely. We process your text locally whenever possible, and any data sent to our servers is encrypted and never stored permanently. We do not sell or share your writing data with third parties under any circumstances.

What's the difference between free and premium versions?

+

The free version covers basic grammar and spelling corrections. Premium unlocks advanced features like style improvements, tone detection, clarity analysis, vocabulary enhancement, and plagiarism checking.

Can I use it for academic writing?

+

Yes! Our premium version includes citation formatting for APA, MLA, Chicago, and other academic styles. It also features a plagiarism checker to ensure your academic work is original and properly cited.
