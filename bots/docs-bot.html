Frequently Asked Questions

Is this extension compatible with other Google Workspace apps?

+

Yes! While optimized for Google Docs, our extension also provides similar functionality in Google Sheets and Google Slides, with features tailored to each application's specific needs.

Can I use the bot for collaborative documents?

+

Absolutely. The Google Docs Bot works seamlessly in collaborative documents. Each user with the extension installed can see suggestions, while those without it won't have their experience affected.

How accurate is the citation generator?

+

Our citation generator follows the latest guidelines for APA, MLA, Chicago, and other academic styles. It's regularly updated to ensure accuracy and compliance with the most recent citation standards.

Does the bot access my document content?

+

The Google Docs Bot processes your document content locally within your browser for most features. Some advanced features may require secure cloud processing, but we never store your document content and maintain strict privacy practices.

What languages are supported?

+

The extension supports writing assistance for English, Spanish, French, German, and Italian. The translation feature supports over 50 languages for document content translation.
